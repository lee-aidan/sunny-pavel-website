hello, my name is sunny.

I was born and raised in Bolinas, a small coastal town in California, and that landscape helped shape my music. I strive to write evocative, meaningful originals that feel natural and relatable. As a primarily solo acoustic guitarist and vocalist, I love pairing meaningful lyrics with corresponding guitar work to create vivid imagery.

My style has been deeply shaped by artists such as John Mayer, Coldplay, James Taylor, and Dire Straits. When I perform, my goal is to create an intimate atmosphere through smooth vocals and detailed playing. In my sets, I blend a mix of my own original songs with unique, stripped-back arrangements of the classics we all know and love.

MUSIC REPERTOIRE

I’ve built a repertoire that is versatile and wide-ranging. I love the folk and singer-songwriter staples from James Taylor, Neil Young, and Fleetwood Mac, and lean into some modern favorites by John Mayer, Coldplay, Jack Johnson, and more. I also draw classics from the likes of Elton John, Van Morrison, Amy Winehouse, The Beatles.

I aim to deliver a professional, engaging performance tailored to diverse audiences and event needs. I curate setlists to create an accessible and feel-good atmosphere. Utilizing the familiar classics, mixing in modern tunes, and including originals to keep the performance fresh.

SELF-SUFFICIENT SOUND

To make things as easy as possible for venues and hosts, I am a fully self-contained act. I provide my own professional sound system (PA), including all microphones, stands, cables, and mixing equipment. This ensures a quick, seamless setup and high-quality, reliable sound for any space—from intimate lounges to outdoor events.
